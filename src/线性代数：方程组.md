###  Linear Equations

- Column picture
- Matrix form

Example:
$$
\begin{cases}
 \ 2x - y = 0\\
 \ -x + 2y = 3
\end{cases}
$$

- Row picture

- Matrix form
  $$
  \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 
    2 &    -1 \\ 
    -1 &    2 \\
  \end{array}\right]
  \left[\begin{array}{cccc} 
    x \\ 
    y \\
  \end{array}\right] = 
  \left[\begin{array}{cccc}
    0 \\
    3 \\
  \end{array}\right]
  $$

  $$
  Ax = b
  $$
- Column picture

$$
\begin{cases}
x + 2y + z = 2 \\
3x + 8y + z = 12 \\
4y + z = 2
\end{cases}
$$
